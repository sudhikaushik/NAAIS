Who We Are

There are over 700,000 Indian-American and Indian national students studying in North America. We want to help bring them together.

The North American Association of Indian Students is a non-partisan umbrella organization that seeks to represent students of Indian nationality, origin and background. NAAIS functions as an umbrella organisation for India and India-related student societies in the United States and Canada that aims to bring a unified voice of India's diaspora youth, on a single, international platform, promote Indian culture and further the USA-India relationship by fostering a dynamic youth-led leadership ecosystem. We hope to collaborate with many governmental and non-governmental organisations, working passionately towards our goal of fostering a community of Indian students alongside furthering the North-America-India relationship through its students and youth. We strive to facilate a community which allows young Indian diaspora to contribute to India's social, political and economic development both when they are studying and/or working in North America, and if/when they eventually return to India. We also hope to facilitate a dialogue between people who wish to know more about India.

Collectivize and Represent

Become the representative voice of Indian origin students in North America and provide a platform that enables this demographic talent pool to engage with institutions, causes, ideas and people with a united, yet diverse front. As well as to represent further the India-US relationship.

Catalyst to Students

Help those who pursue their education in North America, and support them in their higher education—academically, professionally and personally. We aim to support the Indian youth throughout their educational journey in North America — pre-arrival, during their stay as students and post-graduation as alumni. We help students and alumni caught up in issues and help assist their transition. We aim to support students and alumni in developing their professional skills through offering internships and career advice with the aim of generating skilled professionals.

Society Support and Family

As the Umbrella body of Indian and India-related student societies in North America, we provide a support network for local campus societies by organizing events, awareness drives, financial support and even at universities with sizeable Indian populations, we try to create Indian socieites. It is important to note that we only work with Indian organizations around campuses. We are not the national chapter of any society, only a facilitator.

Promoting India and North America

Develop a unique ecosystem enabling young Indian diaspora to share their global experiences in and with India, thus contributing to India's development. We aim to promote and celebrate Indian culture in North America.
